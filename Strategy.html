Strategy games primarily focus on users in game desion-making skills that result in different outcomes.
These games often require lots of trial and error until you find a winning formula.

Strategy games can vary in theme, but using your brain is a constant. Every descion you make has an impact on the result. For example, Civilation VI is a war game where you command an army. Maintaining a strong army, farming resources, and building cities can be quite the challenge.

Some of these games are so compeitive they become e-sports. Teams take the best of the best and compete for prize money. If being competitive isn't your style, you can play alone or online with your friends. If you want a game that doesn't have constant high octane gameplay this might be for you.
